CEO MESSAGE

ECORBIT is
South Korea’s largest and
best-performing comprehensive

environmental company.

As environmental pollution and climate change worsen with
each passing day, there has been increasing attention on the
environmental industry.
ECORBIT leads South Korea’s ESG industry
as South Korea’s top comprehensive environmental company.

ECORBIT began as a company specializing in public water treatment
in 2004, then acquired various environmental companies, including
industrial water treatment and waste treatment companies,
to become South Korea’s largest comprehensive environmental
company with 21 subsidiary companies and 690 business locations.

In addition, ECORBIT has 200 patents for technology-intensive water
management, circular economy waste management, and more
based on our years of knowledge acquisition and networking to
provide the best technologies to businesses.

As a result, ECORBIT has now firmly established three major
value chains in the environmental industry: water treatment,
waste interim treatment (incineration),
and waste final treatment (landfill).

ECORBIT’s staff work together to provide a safe environment
and an abundant quality of life, not only for this generation
but for future generations as well. We promise to continue
to innovate for all of humanity to enjoy a clean and
hopeful tomorrow.

Please show your interest and support for ECORBIT, the company
that pioneers the future of the Korean environmental industry and
fulfills its social role as a company.

Thank you.

CEO In Ho Choi
