TSK water, donation 12 million won to the Community
Chest of Korea in Gyeonggi Province

TSK water donated 12 million won to the Community Chest of Korea in Gyeonggi Province on July 2021. The donation is going to be used for vulnerable local people (nursing homes, childcare centers, living facilities for the disabled, etc.)
