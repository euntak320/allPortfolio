Environmental Management Plan

  • 01

    Provide a comprehensive
    environmental solution
    made for everyone

    ECORBIT provides environmental solutions for various resources, including water treatment, waste, resource recirculation, and new renewable energy, in order to create a satisfactory ESG ecosystem for all stakeholders such as joint businesses and local societies.

  • 02

    Developing an
    environmental culture

    All ECORBIT staff members reduce the use of limited
    resources such as electricity and paper to strive so
    as to maximize the efficiency of resources, while
    also spreading an environmental culture within the
    office through external environmental protection
    activities.

  • 03

    Continuous investment
    for
    eco-friendly
    innovation

    ECORBIT continues to invest for a sustainable future in addition to developing new eco-friendly
    industries.

  • 04

    Establishing trust in
    local societies

    ECORBIT fulfills its social responsibilities by
    communicating with local societies, and establishes
    trust by participating in local cultural events and
    improving the local
    environment.
