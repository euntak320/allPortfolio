01 Corruption

prevention
ECORBIT staff members abide by all laws related to
bribe acceptance or corruption during work.
02 Compliance

within partner
companie
All partner companies with business transactions
with ECORBIT are advised to follow ECORBIT’s compliance policy.
03 Internal
regulation

system
All ECORBIT’s expenditures are appropriately approved and
accurately recorded to the financial ledger.
04 Fair trade

compliance
ECORBIT prohibits unfair trade conduct that discourages competition,
such as bid-rigging.
05 Spread
compliance
culture
To spread compliant culture, ECORBIT conducts
regular training and promotions, and ECORBIT staff members pledge
to observe compliance measures.
06 Protect

whistleblowers
ECORBIT guarantees anonymity of whistleblowers
who report a legal violation, and strictly prohibits any penalties to them.
