Landfills

Waste Landfill Business

ECORBIT possesses the largest landfill capacity in Korea, with 8 landfills that provide a safe and sanitary landfill service for industrial,
general, and designated waste that cannot be recycled or incinerated. We strive to operate the most optimized landfill facilities and
prevent environmental pollution by installing air domes and developing landfill technology to minimize effects on the local environment.
