Industrial Water Treatment System

ECORBIT is recognized as Korea's largest industrial water treatment company through
successful design, manufacture, installation, trial testing, performance testing,
and operation management of water treatment facilities in large-scale plants
for important national infrastructure such as nuclear and thermal power plants.

Facility
  • Ultrapure Water
    System
    The Ultrapure Water System is equipped with a membrane filter system, and the optimized equipment, including the advanced MRM system, reverse osmosis membrane, and high-efficiency membrane deionization system, stably produces ultrapure water without chemical regeneration.
  • Condensate
    Polishing System
    The Condensate Polishing System reliably maintains the steam generator that runs even when there is seawater leakage, and prevents deposits from accumulating in the turbine and corrosion due to deposits.
  • Seawater Electro -
    Hypochlorination
    System
    The Seawater Electro-Hypochlorination System prevents marine life in cooling water from depositing on the plant’s heat exchanger and other equipment to prevent the reduction of heat exchange efficiency in the cooling facility and pressure loss of the cooling pipe.
  • Seawater
    Desalination System
    The Seawater Desalination System is a technology made to resolve the worldwide water shortage problem. The best water quality for freshwater is produced based on decades of knowledge and experience from the reverse osmotic process.
  • ZLD (Zero Liquid
    Discharge)
    The Zero Liquid Discharge system is eco-friendly equipment that recirculates resources using the water’s boiling separation to evaporate, concentrate, and condense at the same time with the concentrated evaporation facility that removes highly concentrated organisms and non-degradable inorganic matter, and is effective in influent flow changes.
  • Steam Generator
    Chemical Cleaning
    Tube corrosion and damage are important problems while operating the PWR steam generator. This chemical cleaning process and equipment removes accumulated corrosion materials to mitigate performance loss.
  • Wastewater
    Treatment System
    We provide the most optimal system with various combinations of unit processes, such as coagulating sedimentation, floatation, biological treatment, medium filtration, membrane filtration, absorption, oxidation, and reduction and treatment of sludge that consider the quality of wastewater and the environment.
  • Operation and
    Maintenance
    We provide facility improvement services by diagnosing and managing the system in real time, operating and maintaining the facility, providing technical support, and consulting based on decades of knowledge and experience in online data collection and reports.
