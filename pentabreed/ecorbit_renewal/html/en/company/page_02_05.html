Sterilization

Production and Operation Management of Medical Waste Sterilized Grinder

ECORBIT ENSOL provides an eco-friendly waste treatment method by eliminating risk of infection
by supporting production and operation management of sterilized grinders for hospitals
to self-dispose of medical waste immediately after it is generated.
