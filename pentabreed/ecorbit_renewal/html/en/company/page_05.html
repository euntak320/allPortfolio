Specialty Business (ECORBIT ENGINEERING)

Demineralized · Ultra Pure Water

MDI

Membrane Electro-Deionization

MDI

Membrane Electro-Deionization

MDI consists of an ion exchange resin and an ion exchange membrane. It is a device that is capable of continuously producing high-purity, ultra water/ pure water using a DC power supply. It is an eco-friendly facility that does not generate wastewater and enables continuous operation without interruption because it does not require the regeneration of ion exchange resin.

Performance : nuclear power plant, thermal power plant, Pure water manufacturing equipment for semiconductor
