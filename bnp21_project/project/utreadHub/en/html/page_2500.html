uCustoms
Introduction
Electronic Export and Import Clearances for Customs Brokers

eCustoms Frame is a customs clearance service to be provided online by KTNET and the Korea Customs Brokers Association. It will facilitate customs clearance on the existing VAN/EDI environment as well as the Internet and lay the groundwork for the consolidated platform of trade, clearance and logistics. This new concept will help customs brokers conduct clearance operations on the Internet and enhance connectivity with the existing system in order to enable information sharing. This portal will integrate KTNET's existing systems for clearance, trade and logistics to provide upgraded services.

Service Details
  • · Customs Clearance for Export and Import / Refund Service / Information Inquiry / Printing Service: Information on export and import declaration, refund request and others can be searched and printed. Real-Time Alert Service for Trading Companies, Forwarders and Customs Brokers through SMS, FAX, e-Mail, and WebMail.
  • · Real-Time Request for Clearance through I/V, P/L and Cargo Manifest
  • · Real-Time Provision of Licensing Information through the connection with the ASPLine , is the service for licensing and confirmation institutions
  • · Transmission of Export Declaration to Forwarders
  • · Inquiry into Logistics Information through the Connection with MFCS
  • · Inquiry through the Connection with uTradeHub Portal
  • · Real-Time Notification of Inbound Goods to Bonded Area
  • · Integrated Inquiry into Shipment Information for Exports and Cargo Tracking Information
