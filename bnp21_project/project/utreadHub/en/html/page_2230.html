e-B/L Services(Logistics Company)
Introduction

The e-B/L Service enables electronic receipt of shipping instruction from trading companies and issuance of an e-B/L.Shipping lines or forwarders without their own exclusive systems are able to use the service by accessing the uTradeHub on the Internet.

Main Functions
S/R Receipt and B/L Issuance

The service provides functions to issue an e-B/L based on the from the shipper. It also provides functions to download the shipping request from the shipper.

B/L Issuance History

This service allows users to search for the list of e-B/L issued by carriers.

Request for B/L Reissue and Receipt of the Request

This enables trading companies to confirm requests for e-B/L correction from the shipper and reissue the e-B/L.

Expected Benefits
  • · Automated digital issuance of B/L will reduce burdens and costs of keeping originals and paper documents.
  • · The service will cut back costs of system configuration and maintenance that companies face when creating their own systems.
