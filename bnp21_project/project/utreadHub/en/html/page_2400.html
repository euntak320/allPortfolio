uTradeSearch
Introduction

The uTradeSearch system is an integrated search portal designed to enable a one-stop search for trade services and marketing information. It consolidates domestic information of trade networks, allowing users to search for expertise and detailed information. Any user who wants trade-related information can access the service without any limit.

Service Details
Characteristics of the Service
  • · Accessible through the uTradeHub and the independent URL(www.utradesearch.or.kr).
  • · Provision of integrated trade data linked with public and private sectors.
  • · Information sharing with companies that have expertise in marketing information.
  • · Integrated and Detailed search for trade information regarding each related institution.
  • · Provision of linked information for each information category
