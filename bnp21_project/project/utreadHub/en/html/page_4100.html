Introduction

The Korean government and trade industry have actively worked with overseas trading industries and trade-related institutions, governments, and international organizations in a bid to achieve a seamless connectivity of international e-Trade, leading global partnership through the uTradeHub. These activities include:

  • · e-Trade Standards Development and Compliance
  • · The Project to Build a Global e-Trade Network
  • · International Cooperation for e-Trade Promotion
  • · e-Trade PR and Consulting Services
