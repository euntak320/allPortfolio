PR and Consulting Services

Based on its outstanding e-Trade system and rich experience in establishing such a leading service, Korea has endeavored to promote the content and expertise of this cutting-edge e-Trade service overseas in order to export the advanced system.

PR Activities

The country has shared its experiences and lessons learned from the creation of e-Trade services with other nations. Examples of some recent efforts are listed below:

  • · APEC Symposium on the Assessment and Benchmark of Paperless Trading (China, 2005)
  • · APEC e-Commerce Business Alliance Forum (China, 2006)
  • · APEC Electronic Trade (Paperless Trading) Training Courses (Providing Four Courses between 2006 and 2007 in China)
  • · The e-Government Roadshow for Latin American Countries (Chile, Columbia, Guatemala, Mexico, Panama, and Peru in 2006 and 2007)
  • · The Middle-East e-Government Roadshow (Iran, Egypt, and Oman in 2006)
  • · The UN 7th Global Forum on Reinventing Government (Austria, 2007)
  • · The United Nations Economic and Social Commission for Western Asia (UN-ESCWA)(Egypt and Jordan, 2007)
  • · The United Nations Economic and Social Commission for Asia and the Pacific (UN-ESCAP)(Thailand, 2007)
Customs Administrations Modernization Proejcts

Korea, which successfully transformed its customs administration to a fully automatic, electronic process, has provided relevant consulting services to other countries. It successfully completed the first phase of the modernization project in October 2005 for Kazakhstan and has entered the bid to proceed with the second phase project and constantly exported e-Trade technologies.

Development and Commercialization of e-Trade Solutions.

The nation is supplying u-TradeHub compatible e-Trade solutions to overseas organizations and companies.
