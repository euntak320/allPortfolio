Service Details
MFCS Service

The system supports consolidation and submission of cargo manifests (the consolidated list of cargo loaded in a ship or a plane) from forwarders and charterers on behalf of shipping lines or airlines in order to control the total volume of inbound and outbound cargos.

Functions of MFCS
  • · Consolidation of Cargo ManifestsThe system consolidates the Master B/L from shipping lines and airlines and House B/L from forwarders and submits the consolidated manifest to the Customs.
  • · Information Sharing between Logistics Companies The MFCS service enables logistics companies to check on the entire process from the consolidation of cargo manifests to customs inspection in real time and to share the information with bonded warehouses and carriers.
Service Details
  • · Maritime AMS: The service enables shipping companies and forwarders to submit cargo reports to the US Customs, 24 hours prior to cargo shipment.
  • · Air AMS: The Service relays cargo reports from forwarders to airlines when required by the US Customs prior to shipment.
e-D/O

e-D/O implements online processing of issuance processes, including the issuance of delivery order (D/O) needed for the delivery of maritime imports.

e-B/L

The service enables issuance of an e-B/L upon receipt of shipping instruction from trading companies.

Air APIS

This helps to submit passenger/crew lists of inbound / outbound flights to the Korea Customs Office and the Immigration Office of the Ministry of Justice.

Air GD

This service is designed to support the submission of arrival and departure reports for inbound / outbound flights.
