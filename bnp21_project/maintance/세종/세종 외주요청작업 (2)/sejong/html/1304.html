내용소개

Central Park, a landmark of Songdo, is a massive recreational space in the city center equipped with many options for water sports, including kayaks, boats, and water taxis, a hanok village with cute cafes and hotels in hanok buildings, the observation deck at G-Tower, and the Tri-Bowl exhibition space.

Central Park at night is a far cry from its calm, peaceful self in daytime. Brilliant lights stream from the nearby buildings to illuminate the park, creating one of the best views of Incheon at night.
